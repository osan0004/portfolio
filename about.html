Hi! I'm David.

I'm a portrait/street photographer & videographer based in Ottawa Ontario. I've always seen a certain beauty in black and white art, I find it represents a person in their truest form with all their perfections and imperfections, which comes together to create something truly beautiful and meaningful.

Currently studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, which is where I'm able to learn and improve my skills in photography and videography. Out of these fields I am also a UX designer and a Web developer.

I've discovered that establishing a connection with my clients or subjects and gaining a deep understanding of the context and purpose behind the images we're creating is the key to producing meaningful work. Consequently, I prioritize quality over quantity in my projects.
